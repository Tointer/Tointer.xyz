Sling Pucky

Sling Pucky is inspired by board game "Sling Puck", where you should use sling rope to slam all pucks to the opponent side

Features:

Two Players Mode

Play the game as it supposed to be played with your friend in two players mode

Arcade singleplayer mode

If no friends available at the moment, try arcade mode, which generates level by level randomly until difficulty gets to high. Don't worry, there are no winning condition, the main goal is to score as high as you can

Rich customization

When you playing arcade mode, there is some chance that you get coins at every level. Use them to make your game visuals match your style!
